# Risks

- [HIGH] **Regulatory compliance**: EdTech regulations vary widely by jurisdiction; data-privacy rules (COPPA, GDPR) add compliance burden for student data.
- [MEDIUM] **Tutor supply quality**: Onboarding and vetting quality tutors at scale is operationally hard; low quality erodes trust quickly.
- [LOW] **Student churn after goal completion**: Students may cancel once they achieve their learning goal, limiting lifetime value.
